# Game Type Instructions

<FULL_COURT>
Standard full-court basketball rules apply. Scoring is 2 and 3 points.
</FULL_COURT>

<THREE_X_THREE>
Official FIBA 3x3 rules apply. Played on a half-court. Scoring is 1 and 2 points. Play is continuous - 'check ball' at the top of the arc after dead balls.
</THREE_X_THREE>

<STREET_BALL>
Streetball/Park rules apply. Usually half-court. Scoring is 1 and 2 points. High emphasis on individual dribble moves and physical play.
</STREET_BALL>

<ONE_X_ONE>
1-on-1 isolation play. Scoring is 1 and 2 points. Focus on offensive player vs single defender.
</ONE_X_ONE>

# Identity Mode Instructions

<JERSEY_COLORS>
Identify teams primarily by their jersey colors. Assign TEMP_TEAM_1 and TEMP_TEAM_2 based on these colors.
</JERSEY_COLORS>

<INTERACTION_BASED>
Players may not have matching jerseys. Identify teams by observing interaction patterns: players who pass to each other, set screens for each other, or celebrate together are teammates. Group players into Group A and Group B based on these dynamic social interactions.
</INTERACTION_BASED>
