# Strategic Roadmap

The long-term vision for StatVision focuses on democratizing pro-level analytics for amateur and pickup sports.

## 🏀 The "Amateur Experience"
- **Ghost Scoreboard:** Automatic digital overlay on raw video based on AI detections.
- **Highlight Generator:** One-click social media "mixtape" creation.
- **Visual Roster Pairing:** Identifying players by shoes, hair, or physical traits for games without jerseys.

## ⚙️ Technical Edge
- **Edge Pre-processing:** Mobile-side compression and frame extraction to save bandwidth.
- **Multi-Angle Syncing:** Combining multiple phone recordings into a 360-degree view.
- **Halftime Reports:** Ultra-low latency processing for mid-game strategic summaries.
- **Pose Estimation:** Analyzing shooting mechanics (elbow flare, jump height) for form coaching.

## 🌱 Growth & Community
- **Evolution Dashboard:** Tracking long-term skill improvements (the "1% better every day" goal).
- **Player Archetypes:** AI-driven classification (e.g., "Glass Cleaner," "Floor General").
- **Park Heatmaps:** Aggregating stats across a specific physical court to identify "local legends".
- **Automated Referee:** AI-flagging of potential violations (Travels, Double Dribbles) to assist in pickup game disputes.
